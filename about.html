About TechShop KH

Established in 2015

TechShop KH started as a small computer repair shop in Phnom Penh and has grown to become one of Cambodia's most trusted technology retailers.

We specialize in providing high-quality laptops, desktops, and accessories from world-renowned brands, backed by genuine warranties and excellent customer service.

Our Mission

To make technology accessible to everyone in Cambodia by offering premium products at competitive prices with unmatched customer support.
